Our  Core Offerings 

Community-Based Fire Risk Assessments

Participatory evaluation of fire hazards and vulnerabilities in local communities. Work alongside residents, community leaders, and local researchers to co-create assessments that reflect lived experience and local knowledge.

Know More

Support to Humanitarian and Development Partners

We provide expert support to humanitarian and development partners, helping them integrate fire safety and risk reduction into their programs and operations.

Know More

Fire Experiments & Scientific Tools

We conduct innovative fire experiments and develop scientific tools to better understand fire behavior. Our research helps inform fire prevention strategies and builds a more resilient future.

Know More

Research & Advocacy

We conduct research and advocate for policies and practices that reduce fire risk and protect communities. Our work informs decision-makers and builds a more fire-resilient world.

Know More

Training & Learning

We deliver training and learning programs to empower individuals and organizations with the knowledge and skills to assess and respond to fire risks.
